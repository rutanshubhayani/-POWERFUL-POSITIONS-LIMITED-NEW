Membership Terms

POWERFUL POSITION GYM offers various membership options designed to meet your fitness goals. All memberships are subject to the following terms:

  • Minimum Age Requirement: Members must be at least 16 years old. Members under 18 require parental consent.
  • Membership Activation: Your membership begins on the date of enrollment and payment processing.
  • Membership Types: Choose from monthly, quarterly, or annual membership plans.
  • Access Hours: 24/7 access for premium members, standard hours for basic members.
  • Guest Privileges: Members may bring guests subject to facility capacity and guest fees.

All members must complete a health and fitness assessment before beginning any training program. Medical clearance may be required for certain programs or conditions.

Membership benefits include access to all equipment, group classes, locker facilities, and basic amenities. Premium services such as personal training, nutrition consultations, and specialized programs may require additional fees.

Facility Usage Guidelines

To ensure a safe and enjoyable experience for all members, please observe the following facility guidelines:

  • Equipment Usage: Use equipment safely and as intended. Report any damaged equipment immediately.
  • Cleanliness: Wipe down equipment after use and maintain personal hygiene standards.
  • Dress Code: Appropriate athletic attire and closed-toe shoes are required at all times.
  • Time Limits: Respect time limits on cardio equipment during peak hours.
  • Spotting: Always use spotters for heavy lifting and request assistance when needed.
  • Noise Levels: Keep conversations at a respectful volume and avoid dropping weights.
Safety First

Your safety is our priority. Follow all posted safety guidelines, use equipment properly, and immediately report any injuries or incidents to staff members.

POWERFUL POSITION GYM reserves the right to deny facility access to any individual who fails to comply with these guidelines or whose behavior compromises the safety or experience of other members.

Payment & Billing

POWERFUL POSITION GYM offers flexible payment options to accommodate your financial preferences:

  • Payment Methods: We accept credit cards, debit cards, bank transfers, and cash payments.
  • Billing Cycle: Monthly memberships are billed on the same date each month.
  • Auto-Renewal: Memberships automatically renew unless cancelled according to our policy.
  • Late Fees: A £25 late fee applies to overdue payments after a 5-day grace period.
  • Failed Payments: Multiple failed payment attempts may result in membership suspension.
Payment Policy

All fees are non-refundable except as specifically provided in our cancellation policy. Membership rates are subject to change with 30 days advance notice.

Annual memberships paid in full receive a discount. Personal training sessions and additional services are charged separately and must be paid in advance or at the time of service.

Cancellation Policy

We understand that circumstances change, and we've designed a fair cancellation policy:

  • Notice Period: 30 days written notice required for all membership cancellations.
  • Cancellation Methods: Submit requests in person, by email, or certified mail.
  • Processing Time: Cancellations become effective at the end of the current billing cycle.
  • Freezing Options: Temporary membership freeze available for medical reasons or extended travel.
  • No Refunds: Unused portions of paid memberships are generally non-refundable.
Membership Freeze

Freeze your membership for £10/month for up to 6 months for medical reasons, extended travel, or other qualifying circumstances. Medical freezes require documentation.

Emergency cancellations due to serious medical conditions or military deployment may qualify for special consideration. Please contact our member services team to discuss your specific situation.
